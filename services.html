Pricing & Availability

Prices vary by job, genre, book length, time of year, and services needed. As this press is our own passion project, availability may vary based on our interest. (Primary interests are fiction projects in the fantasy and sci-fi genres, all ages.)

Note: We will not publish your book for you. We can support you through all stages of the manuscript development process and as you upload your book to various POD services, but you will maintain complete control of your copyright, revenue, ads, and promotions and qualify as an official 'indie author.'
