Our Services

Comprehensive publishing support for indie authors

Manuscript Editing

Professional editing services including developmental editing, copy editing, and proofreading. We work closely with authors to refine their manuscripts while preserving their unique voice.

What's included:

  • Developmental editing
  • Line editing
  • Copy editing
  • Proofreading

Cover Design

Eye-catching cover designs that capture your book's essence and appeal to your target audience. We create custom designs that stand out on shelves and online.

What's included:

  • Custom cover design
  • Front, back, and spine design
  • Multiple design concepts
  • Revisions until you're satisfied

Interior Layout & Typesetting

Beautiful, readable interior layouts that enhance the reading experience. We ensure your book looks professional and is easy to read.

What's included:

  • Professional typesetting
  • Chapter design
  • Typography selection
  • Print-ready files

ISBN & Distribution

We help you obtain ISBNs and set up distribution channels so your book reaches readers through major retailers and independent bookstores.

What's included:

  • ISBN acquisition
  • Distribution setup
  • Retailer listings
  • Ongoing support

Marketing Support

Guidance on marketing your book effectively. We provide resources and strategies to help you reach your audience and build your author platform.

What's included:

  • Marketing strategy consultation
  • Social media guidance
  • Press release templates
  • Author website recommendations

Publishing Packages

Complete publishing packages that bundle multiple services at a discounted rate. Perfect for authors who want comprehensive support from start to finish.

What's included:

  • All editing services
  • Cover and interior design
  • ISBN and distribution
  • Marketing support
